Field Description
Amount (required) If the transaction is a money transfer, the amount must be less than or equal to $2,500 (USD), except for Singapore and Australia $15,000 (USD) (domestic limit), and Bahrain, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Kuwait, Macedonia, Morocco, Nigeria, Qatar, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Serbia, Ukraine, and United Arab Emirates $50,000 (USD) (domestic limit). If the transaction is a funds disbursement, pre-paid load, or credit card bill pay, the amount must be less than or equal to $50,000USD.
Transfer click to transfer the amount to recipient.
AFT(AccountFundingTransactions) AFT check box will display the request and response for AFT.It will also display the request header along with X-Pay_Token
OCT (OriginalCreditTransactions) OCT check box will display the request and response for OCT.It will also display the request header along with X-Pay_Token
